GiveMeMyHealthData

privacy policy · last updated: may 8, 2026

the short version

this app does not collect your data. period.

what the app does

GiveMeMyHealthData reads data from Apple Health on your device and converts it into CSV files. these files are saved locally on your device in the app's Documents folder. that is the entire scope of what this app does.

what data the app accesses

the app requests read-only access to Apple Health categories that you explicitly approve. you choose what to export. the app never writes data to Apple Health.

where your data goes

nowhere.

  • your health data is read from Apple Health and written to CSV files on your device
  • CSV files are stored in the app's Documents folder on your phone
  • no data is sent to any server
  • no data is transmitted over the internet
  • no data is shared with the developer or any third party
  • no data leaves your device unless you personally choose to share the exported files (for example, via AirDrop)

data collection

this app collects zero data. specifically:

  • no personal information
  • no health information
  • no usage analytics
  • no crash reporting
  • no device identifiers
  • no cookies
  • no tracking pixels
  • no advertising identifiers

third-party services

this app uses zero third-party services. no analytics platforms, no ad networks, no cloud storage, no anything.

accounts

this app does not require or support user accounts. there is nothing to sign up for.

children's privacy

this app does not collect data from anyone, including children.

data retention

the app does not retain your data. CSV files exist only in the app's Documents folder on your device. you can delete them at any time through the Files app.

data security

your health data is processed entirely on your device using Apple's HealthKit framework. since no data is transmitted or stored externally, there is no risk of server-side data breach. local file security is handled by your device's built-in encryption and passcode protection.

your rights

you control everything:

  • you choose which health categories to export
  • you choose when to export
  • you can revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health
  • you can delete exported CSV files at any time
  • you can delete the app at any time and all app data is removed

changes to this policy

if this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. given that the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
